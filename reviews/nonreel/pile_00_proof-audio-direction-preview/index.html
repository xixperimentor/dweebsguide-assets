what changed

direction i am proposing now

  • all narration stays in third-person brand voice.
  • every proof family gets audio, including stats assets.
  • target most scripts to land around 30 to 60 seconds, with most examples in this pack sitting closer to 35 to 50 seconds.
  • the voice should explain why the proof matters, not just read the card text back.
  • no fake member impressions, no diary acting, and no invented scene detail.
how to review it

best qc method

  • first check whether the third-person rule holds in every card.
  • then check whether the audio adds meaning rather than just rereading the asset.
  • then check whether the length feels justified for the visual format.
  • for stats cards, focus on whether the narration earns the extra screen time.

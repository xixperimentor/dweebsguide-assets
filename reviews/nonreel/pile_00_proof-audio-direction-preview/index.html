what changed

direction i am proposing now

  • all narration stays in third-person brand voice.
  • every proof family gets audio, including stats assets.
  • use standard onyx as the default voice for this preview pack.
  • keep scripts consumer-facing and target most reads to land around 30 to 60 seconds, with most examples in this pack closer to 35 to 50 seconds.
  • the voice should explain why the proof matters, not just read the screen text back.
  • no fake member impressions, no diary acting, and no invented scene detail.
how to review it

best qc method

  • first check whether every script sounds like real consumer-facing narration.
  • then check whether the third-person rule holds in every example.
  • then check whether the audio adds meaning rather than just rereading the screen text.
  • for stats examples, focus on whether the narration earns the extra screen time.

Testimonial 35 to 50 seconds 145 to 155 wpm these should sound like calm proof summaries. the voice is there to translate the result into something believable and repeatable. 1. name the visible result

4. close with a brief brand-proof anchor
Stats Grid 30 to 45 seconds 145 to 155 wpm stats still get narration, but the voice should support the numbers rather than compete with them. 1. name the headline
2. explain what the numbers prove

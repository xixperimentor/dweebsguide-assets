I remember a time when I was really into a girl after just a couple of great dates.

I got so caught up in the excitement that I started assuming we were on the same page. Turns out, I was just creating my own narrative. One evening, I finally decided to ask her how she felt about where things were heading. That simple question opened the door to a real conversation. We shared our thoughts, and it felt like a weight lifted off both our shoulders. Looking back, I realized that clarity is everything in the early stages. If you’re feeling uncertain, just speak up. It’s better to know where you both stand than to guess and risk misreading the whole situation.

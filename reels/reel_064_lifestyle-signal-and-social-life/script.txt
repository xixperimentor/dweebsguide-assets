A while back, I found myself swiping endlessly, hoping each match would fill this emptiness I felt.

I was hung up on finding romance, but I didn't realize I was confusing that need with my craving for connection. I was isolated and desperate, and honestly, it showed. Then one day, I decided to take a step back. I reached out to an old friend and started investing time in my social life. I began going out more, joining a few community events, just making an effort to feel connected. It changed everything. With a solid group of friends, I felt more grounded and secure. Now, when I date, it’s not from a place of desperation. Instead, I go in with confidence, knowing I'm not relying on anyone to fill a void. Focus on building those connections; you'll find everything else follows.

I remember this one date I had.

We laughed a lot, and I felt that spark, you know? But as the night went on, I realized our humor didn’t quite sync up. I’d crack a joke, and she’d just... look at me. It was like pulling teeth trying to keep the energy alive. Afterwards, I caught myself wanting to see her again, even though I felt drained. It hit me—just because there's chemistry doesn't mean we should mesh well. I had to check in with myself and ask, “Does this connection fit into my life?” Now, I always remind myself to look beyond the excitement and really pay attention to how we interact. If it feels like work, it probably isn’t right. Don’t ignore those signs. Trust your instincts.

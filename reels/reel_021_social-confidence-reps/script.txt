There was a time when I felt completely paralyzed in social situations, especially when I was trying to meet someone new.

I’d overthink everything, convinced I needed to feel totally ready before making a move. But the truth is, that just kept me stuck. One night, I tried a different approach. Instead of waiting for the perfect moment, I just focused on going through some simple interactions—a quick hello, a light-hearted comment, even just sharing my thoughts on a TV show. That small shift opened up a whole new world for me. Now I tell guys to stop waiting for confidence to magically appear. Just start small. Build up those little moments. They’ll add up and help you feel way more comfortable over time. Trust me; it really works.

I remember this time when I was really into a girl who just didn’t seem that into me.

I’d text her, make plans, and always end up trying to convince her to engage. It felt like I was doing everything right, but deep down, I started to realize I was just forcing something that wasn’t there. One day, I took a step back and noticed how much effort I was putting in versus what I was getting back. I decided it was time to have a straight conversation instead of chasing signals that were faint at best. What I learned is that sometimes stepping away is the most powerful move you can make. If the connection isn’t mutual, it’s alright to walk away and focus on someone who matches your energy. Don’t let pride keep you stuck in a one-sided game.

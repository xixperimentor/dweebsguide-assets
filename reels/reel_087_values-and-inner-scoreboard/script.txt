I remember a time when I was seeing someone who clearly wasn’t treating me right.

I kept dismissing the red flags because I wanted to make it work. I thought I was being understanding, but deep down, I was betraying my own values. It hit me one evening when I noticed I was feeling more anxious than excited about our time together. That’s when I decided enough was enough. I walked away from that situation. It was tough, but I felt a weight lift off my shoulders. If I could tell another guy something, it’d be this: don’t ignore those feelings that say you’re not being treated right. Trust yourself. Stand by the standards you believe in. You’ll find your confidence grows when you do.

I remember this one night out with a woman I thought was really cool.

We hit it off, both joking around and laughing. But I noticed something was off. I was so caught up in trying to be funny that I stopped being real. Instead of just letting the moment breathe, I kept throwing jokes out as a shield. Then, halfway through the evening, I paused. I decided to drop the act for a second and just told her, “I really enjoy spending time with you.” It totally changed the energy. We shifted from playful banter to a genuine connection. So, if you're in that same spot, don't shy away from being honest. It's okay to mix humor with sincerity. Trust me, it creates a deeper vibe that she’ll definitely appreciate.

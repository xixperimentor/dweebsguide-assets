A while back, I was out on a date, and I really wanted to impress this woman.

I went all out—trying to be charming, telling stories, and pushing the conversation. But I noticed that I was talking way more than I was listening. It hit me later that I wasn’t creating any real connection. So, I took a step back. I started focusing on what she was saying, making it a two-way street. I learned to communicate more openly and not rush things. If I could give you a tip, it’d be this: focus on being reliable and genuine in your interactions. Build trust first. That way, you can forge something much deeper than just a flashy first impression.

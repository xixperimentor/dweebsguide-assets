I remember dating someone a while back who had this amazing energy.

It was intense and fun, but I quickly realized that wasn’t enough. We’d make plans, and then she’d flake last minute or just go quiet for days. At first, I was all about the highs, but it started to wear me down. I needed to know I could rely on her, and that consistency wasn’t there. I learned to pay attention to how often she followed through, instead of just chasing that initial thrill. If I could give any advice, it’d be this: look for those small, steady actions that show you can trust someone. The real connection comes from knowing they’ll be there, not just in the fun moments, but in the everyday ones too.

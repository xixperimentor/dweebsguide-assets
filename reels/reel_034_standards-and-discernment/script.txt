There was this girl I really liked.

We had an undeniable chemistry, but as we got deeper into it, I noticed she often ghosted me for days. At first, I brushed it off, thinking maybe she was just busy. I kept making excuses. But after the third time it happened, I had to face the reality. I realized she didn’t value my time. It was hard to accept, especially when things felt intense between us. So I cut my losses and focused on what I needed – respect and consistency. If I could tell another guy anything, it would be this: don’t ignore the signs just because the sparks are flying. Pay attention, and don't let hope cloud your judgment.

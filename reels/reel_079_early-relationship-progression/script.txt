I remember jumping into a relationship way too fast after a couple of great dates.

Everything felt electric, so I started rearranging my life around her. I thought being available all the time would make her feel cherished. Instead, it took a toll on my own sense of self. I realized that by losing my own rhythm, I was dimming the spark between us. So, I took a step back. I focused on my own routines while still being there for her. Now, I know it’s not about merging lives completely but about supporting each other while still standing as individuals. I’d tell any man: it’s okay to be present without losing yourself. Keep your life intact, and the connection stays strong.

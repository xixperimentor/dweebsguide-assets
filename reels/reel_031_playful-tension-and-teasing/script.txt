I remember a date that started off great, but somewhere along the way, I got too caught up in trying to impress her with these flirty lines I’d seen online.

I thought I was being charming, but it just felt forced, and I could tell she wasn’t really engaged. Then I caught myself drifting from the moment. I decided to switch gears. Instead of sticking to my script, I began picking up on her humor and the things she said. I threw in a playful jab about her favorite movie and it landed perfectly. Looking back, I realized that being present and responding to her vibe was way more effective. If I could give advice to anyone, it would be to let the energy of the moment guide your flirting. Real connection happens when you’re genuinely tuned in.

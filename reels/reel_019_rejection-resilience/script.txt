There was a time when I went out with someone I was really excited about.

We had a great connection, but she ghosted me after just a couple of dates. At first, I felt this wave of bitterness wash over me, thinking, "What’s the point? Women are all the same." But then I took a step back. I started recognizing that my frustration wasn’t helping me at all; it was just leading to a closed-off version of myself. Instead of accepting that as my reality, I began to focus on the positives — what I learned from that experience. So, if you find yourself in a similar situation, don’t let one setback define your outlook on dating. Embrace the uncertainty and keep your heart open. Trust me, it's worth it.

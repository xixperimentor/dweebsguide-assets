I once got swept up in a whirlwind with someone who seemed perfect on the surface.

I was so caught up in the excitement that I overlooked some red flags. I realized my standards were blurry, and I was just chasing the thrill, ignoring things that truly mattered. After that experience, I took a step back. I wrote down what I wanted from a partner—like consistency, honesty, and respect. It helped me see things clearly. I became more aware of how her actions didn’t always match her words. To any guy out there, get specific about what you value before you dive in. It’ll save you from a lot of confusion and heartache down the road. Trust me, knowing what you want makes all the difference.

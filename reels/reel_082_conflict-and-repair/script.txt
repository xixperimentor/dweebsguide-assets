I remember a time I had a misunderstanding with a woman I was seeing.

She brought up something I did that upset her, and my first instinct was to jump in and defend myself. I felt accused, like I had to justify my actions right away. But then I paused and really thought about it. I realized I was so focused on protecting my pride that I missed the chance to understand her feelings. So, I took a breath and asked her to explain what she meant. That simple question opened the door for a real conversation, and it changed everything. If you find yourself in a similar situation, try this: don’t rush to defend, just listen. You might just uncover something valuable.

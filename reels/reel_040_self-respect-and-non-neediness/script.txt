I remember a time when I was really into this woman, and I just couldn't read her signals.

One minute, she seemed interested, and the next, she was distant. I found myself hanging on her every word, craving her approval. It was exhausting and honestly, pretty pathetic. Then I had a realization. I was letting her mood dictate my own feelings. So, I shifted my focus. Instead of chasing her responses, I decided to define what I wanted from our interactions—clarity and respect. When I started walking away from those low-energy exchanges, I felt a strange sense of relief and strength. So, if you find yourself in a confusing moment, remember: set your own standards and stick to them. Don't let someone else’s vibe determine your worth.

I remember a time when I really liked this girl.

Things were exciting, but as we went out, I noticed she would push on my boundaries. At first, I brushed it off. I thought, “This is just part of the game.” But deep down, I felt unease creeping in. One night, she joked about something I was serious about. I realized that was a red flag. I had to ask myself if I was willing to compromise myself just to keep the spark alive. So, I started to stand firm. When I expressed my limits, I saw her true colors. It hit me hard—if someone can't respect my no, then maybe they’re not worth keeping around. If you’re in a similar spot, pay attention to how they react to your boundaries. That says way more than any charming words ever could.

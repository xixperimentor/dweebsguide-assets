I remember this one time I was getting to know someone, and I thought being the most available guy would win her over.

I kept suggesting outings, always texting first. I wanted her to see how much I cared, but truth is, it just felt off, like I was trying too hard. Then it hit me. She wasn’t putting in the same effort, and I was just giving without thinking about what we were actually building together. So, I dialed it back. I started matching her pace, being warm and present but not overextending myself. If I could share one piece of wisdom, it would be this: be mindful of how much you invest early on. Balance is key, and it shows real respect for both yourself and her.

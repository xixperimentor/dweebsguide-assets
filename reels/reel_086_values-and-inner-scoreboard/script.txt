I remember a time when I was dating someone who would send mixed signals.

One minute she was all in, and the next, she was distant. I kept trying to seek clarity, hoping she'd confirm her feelings, but all I got was frustration. It felt like I was just pleading for respect. Then, I realized I wasn’t honoring my own need for self-respect. So, I stepped back and made a change. I decided to set a boundary. I told her, “If you’re not sure, that’s okay, but I can’t keep waiting.” It felt tough at first, but it felt right. To any guy out there facing something similar, remember that your values should shape your actions, even when it’s uncomfortable. Trust yourself, and don’t compromise on what matters to you.

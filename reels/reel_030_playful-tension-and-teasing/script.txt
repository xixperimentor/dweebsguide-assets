I remember a date where I was trying too hard to be funny and keep the energy high.

I was cracking jokes, but I could feel her pull away. It was confusing because I thought I was being charming, but it just didn’t land. Then I caught myself overexplaining everything instead of enjoying the moment. I took a breath and decided to dial it down. I held her gaze a bit longer, smiled, and let the silence sit between us. That shift changed the vibe entirely. It felt more natural, more relaxed. So if you find yourself in a similar spot, try saying less and just be present. Trust the silence; it can create a spark you didn’t even know was there.

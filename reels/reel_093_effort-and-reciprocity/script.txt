I remember this one girl I was really into.

I’d text her constantly, but I noticed she didn’t respond as quickly as I did. At first, I thought she wasn’t interested, and it kind of messed with my head. Then I started paying attention. She’d always make plans and ask questions when we hung out. It hit me—she was showing up in her way, just different from mine. I was so focused on how we communicated that I forgot to look at the bigger picture. So, I shifted my approach. I stopped measuring our connection by texts and instead noticed all the ways she invested in our time together. If you find yourself in a similar spot, look beyond the surface. Effort doesn’t always show up as you expect, but it’s there if you’re willing to see it.

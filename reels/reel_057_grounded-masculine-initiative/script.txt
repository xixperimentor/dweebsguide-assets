I remember a date where I was so eager to impress, I ended up overwhelming her.

I had this grand plan—dinner at a fancy restaurant followed by a surprise rooftop view. It was all me, and I could see the pressure building in her eyes. She didn't seem excited; she felt trapped. After picking up on her hesitation, I took a step back. Instead of dictating the entire evening, I suggested a simple coffee first, leaving room for her to say yes or no. Suddenly, it felt more relaxed. To any guy out there, I’d say don’t suffocate the moment with intensity. Offer a clear, simple plan, and let her choose. It’s all about making things easier for both of you.

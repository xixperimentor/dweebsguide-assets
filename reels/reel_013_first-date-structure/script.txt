I remember my first date with Jenna.

I went all out, planning this elaborate dinner at a fancy place. It felt like a lot of pressure, and honestly, the vibe was tense from the start. I wanted everything to be perfect, but instead, it just felt forced. After a while, I noticed we were both struggling to connect. So, I decided to change things up. I suggested we grab a coffee and take a stroll instead. Just like that, the mood lightened. We could just talk and let the conversation flow. If I could give advice to another guy, it would be this: keep your plans simple and flexible. Go for something casual where you can easily decide to keep it going or wrap it up. It makes all the difference in building chemistry.

There was a time when I put way too much pressure on my love life.

I was searching for that special connection, but my week was just about work and scrolling on my phone. I realized that the more I chased romance, the more I felt empty inside. Then I took a step back and started filling my life with things that mattered—friends, hobbies, and routines. I began to explore new activities and reconnect with old buddies. That shift was liberating. Suddenly, when I went on dates, I was fully present rather than desperately looking for validation. So, if you’re feeling that emptiness, focus on enriching your life outside of dating first. Build a life you love, and watch how it transforms your connections with others.

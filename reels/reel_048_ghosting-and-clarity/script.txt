I remember this time I connected with a woman online.

We had a great vibe, but then I let my interest fade without saying anything. I thought ghosting would spare her feelings, but instead, it just left her hanging. I noticed she kept messaging, hoping for a response, and it hit me—I’d just created confusion. After that, I made a promise to myself. If I’m not feeling it, I’ll tell the other person directly. It’s much better to offer clear closure than to leave someone wondering what went wrong. So, if you find yourself drifting away from a connection, just be honest. A simple message can save a lot of unnecessary heartache for both of you.

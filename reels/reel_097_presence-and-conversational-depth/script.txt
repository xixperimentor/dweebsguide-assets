I remember this one date where I was so focused on impressing her with my stories that I completely missed the nuances of what she was sharing.

She mentioned a tough day at work, and instead of acknowledging her experience, I just jumped in with a funny story about my own job. I could see it in her eyes—she felt unheard. After that, I took a step back. I started really listening, picking up on the feelings behind her words. The next time she spoke about her day, I asked more about how it made her feel. Suddenly, the conversation deepened, and we connected on a whole new level. So, if you're on a date, just remember: when she shares something, lean in. Reflect on what she’s saying before diving into your own experiences. Trust me; it makes all the difference.

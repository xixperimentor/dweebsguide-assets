A while back, I was seeing someone who was really kind and fun to be around.

We clicked on a lot of levels, but I started to notice that our communication styles just didn’t match. I’d want to talk things out, while she preferred to keep it light. I kept convincing myself that things would improve, thinking I just needed to give it time. But in the end, I realized it wasn’t about her being at fault; it was about us not really fitting together. Now, I make it a point to check for alignment early on. If something feels off, I don’t hesitate to end it instead of dragging it out. I’d tell any guy to trust those instincts. It’s better to step away than to hold on out of a sense of obligation.

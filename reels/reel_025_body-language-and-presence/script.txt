I remember this one date where I really struggled to connect.

I was throwing out lines, but I could feel this weird tension in the air. I was fidgeting and rushing through my words, almost like I was trying to get it over with. It hit me later that my nerves were showing, and I wasn't even aware of it. So, I decided to switch things up. Before my next interaction, I focused on grounding myself. I stood tall, took a breath, and let my voice settle. It felt powerful. Just those few extra seconds of stillness made everything smoother. If I could give any guy a tip, it’d be this: slow down for those initial moments. Own your space, and let the words come naturally. Your presence speaks volumes before you even say a thing.

I remember chatting with a girl on Bumble, and I fired off these quick questions, hoping to keep things lively.

It felt like an interview, and honestly, I could sense her pulling back. I was trying so hard to keep the flow going that I lost sight of the real connection. Then I decided to switch things up. Instead of tossing out a barrage of questions, I led with a thought about her favorite travel spot that caught my eye. I shared why that resonated with me before asking her about her experiences. Suddenly, the vibe lifted. So, if you find yourself in a similar bind, take a step back. Share a piece of yourself first. That way, you create an opening for something real and engaging. It’s all about making that human connection.

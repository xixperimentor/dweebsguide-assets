I remember when I first jumped into online dating.

My profile was a bunch of clichés—just lists of things I liked, like movies and hiking. It felt safe, but I got crickets in return. Then one day, I decided to change things up. Instead of saying I love adventure, I shared a story about hiking up a mountain and almost getting lost. Suddenly, conversations started flowing. What clicked for me was that I wasn’t just sharing facts; I was inviting people into a moment. So, if you’re feeling stuck, think about what gives your life texture. Share opinions or stories that spark a conversation. Give potential matches something to respond to, and watch the magic happen.

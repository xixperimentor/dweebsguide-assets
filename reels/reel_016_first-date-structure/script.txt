I remember a first date where I ended up making things way more complicated than they needed to be.

As we were saying goodbye, I felt that pressure to know exactly how she felt right then. So, instead of just saying I had a great time and would love to grab another drink later, I started fishing for her approval. It was awkward. Looking back, I realized how clarity really matters. The best move was just to keep it simple and warm. I should have stated my interest clearly, then left it at that. So, if you find yourself in that moment, just express your enjoyment and suggest the next step without putting her on the spot. Trust that the next move will come when the time is right.

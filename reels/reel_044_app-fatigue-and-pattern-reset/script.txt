You know, there was a time when I couldn’t stop swiping.

I’d sit there, thumbing through profiles like a mindless robot, thinking the next match would be my golden ticket. But honestly? It just left me feeling numb, disconnected, and overwhelmed. Then I realized I was treating every swipe like it was my last chance. I stepped back and decided to focus on fewer conversations but really engage with those. Instead of juggling ten chats where I barely knew anyone, I narrowed it down to a couple of meaningful ones. The difference was striking. I felt more present and confident in each interaction. If I could tell another guy anything, it would be this: slow down. Invest your energy where it counts, and you’ll find deeper connections.

Not too long ago, I went on a great first date.

We laughed, shared stories, and I felt a real connection. But afterward, I started overthinking everything. I shot her a long message, pouring out my feelings and doubts, hoping for reassurance. Looking back, I realize that was a mistake. Instead of clarity, I just created more confusion. I noticed that when I calmed down and simply sent a brief note saying I enjoyed our time together and wanted to meet again, it changed everything. So, if you ever find yourself lost in mixed signals, keep it simple. Speak your truth, but let it breathe. Give space for her response instead of filling it with pressure. Trust me, it’s more effective than over-explaining.

There was a time I really struggled with a relationship.

I was always the one texting first—always checking in, keeping the conversation alive. It felt like I had to do everything, or she might lose interest. But I never stopped to think about what would happen if I pulled back a bit. So, one day I decided to just let things breathe. I stopped initiating for a few days. At first, it felt uncomfortable, but then I noticed something important. If she wasn’t reaching out on her own, maybe the connection wasn’t as solid as I thought. What I learned is this: if you’re putting in all the effort, it’s crucial to see if she’s willing to meet you halfway. Trust the process. Sometimes, stepping back gives you the clarity you truly need.

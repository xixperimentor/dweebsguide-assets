A while back, I was really struggling with dating.

I'd go on these one-off dates but felt like I had no real life to share. I’d sit across from a woman and realize my conversations were flat because I wasn’t doing much outside of work. Then, I started volunteering at a local shelter. It was a game changer. I met new people, built connections, and had stories to tell that weren’t just about my job. My social skills improved as I became comfortable talking to different folks. So, if you find yourself feeling stuck, consider adding something to your life that gets you out there regularly. Whether it's a class, a hobby, or volunteering, being part of a community will naturally boost your confidence and make you a more interesting guy.

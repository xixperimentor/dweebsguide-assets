I remember this girl I was really into.

We’d been texting, and sometimes she’d take forever to respond. I started spiraling, thinking maybe I’d messed something up or she wasn’t that into me. I was reading way too much into every delay. Then, I stepped back and noticed how she still made plans and showed up consistently. Suddenly, it clicked: her texting style didn’t mean she wasn’t interested. It just meant she was busy, like all of us sometimes are. So, if you find yourself stressing over a slow reply, take a breath. Instead of focusing on the gaps, look at the bigger picture. Are you both still making plans? If so, there’s still something good here.

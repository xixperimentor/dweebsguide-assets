You know, there was this time when I hit it off with a girl.

We had some great conversations, and I was feeling pretty confident. But then, out of nowhere, she went silent. I found myself checking my phone constantly, thinking I could just send one more message to keep the thread alive. Looking back, I realized I was just prolonging the uncertainty. It felt like I was digging a hole, desperately trying to stay relevant rather than owning my feelings. So, I decided to shift gears. I sent a single, straightforward follow-up to check in. That was it. If I could share one piece of advice, it’d be this: shoot your shot once and let it be. If they’re interested, they'll respond. If not, move on with your head held high.

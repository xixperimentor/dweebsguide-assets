I remember a time I was on a date, and things just felt… off.

I was so focused on sticking to my prepared questions that I completely missed the vibe she was giving off. It was like I was just checking boxes instead of actually connecting. Then, something clicked. I noticed how she was leaning forward, her eyes lighting up when she talked about her favorite travel spots. Instead of pushing through my list, I asked her to expand on that. Suddenly, the conversation flowed, and it felt genuine and alive. If I could give one piece of advice, it’d be this: really tune into what’s happening in the moment. Forget the script. Share in the exchange, and you'll find that the best conversations come from real observations, not just rehearsed lines.

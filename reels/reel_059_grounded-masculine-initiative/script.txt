I remember a date I was really excited about.

I had this whole plan mapped out—dinner and then a cool bar afterward. I thought I was being smooth, but halfway through dinner, I could tell she looked a bit nervous. I was so focused on my script that I didn’t pick up on her vibe. Then it hit me. Instead of just pushing forward, I slowed down and asked her what she thought. It turned out she wasn’t into that bar. So I pivoted, and we ended up at this cozy little café instead. If I could talk to another guy about it, I’d say: have a plan, but don’t cling to it. Really tune in to how she’s feeling. That connection matters way more than a perfect itinerary.

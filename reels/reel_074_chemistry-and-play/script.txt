I remember going on a first date where we just sat across from each other at a fancy restaurant.

It felt like an interview, and the tension built with every awkward silence. I could see her growing restless, and honestly, so was I. I realized later I was trying too hard to impress instead of just letting things flow. So, I decided to change it up. I invited her to a local rock-climbing gym. It turned out to be a blast. We were laughing and cheering each other on, and the nervous energy transformed into something fun and genuine. If I could give one piece of advice, it’d be to pick an activity that brings movement into your date. It makes it easier to connect and keeps things light. Trust me, you'll feel the difference.

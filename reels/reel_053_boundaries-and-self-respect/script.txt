I remember a time I was juggling plans with a girl who kept changing her mind last minute.

At first, I thought it was just part of being flexible, but deep down, I felt frustrated. I didn’t want to make a scene, so I kept saying yes, hoping it would eventually get better. Eventually, I realized that I wasn’t being true to myself. I started to understand that I needed to set some ground rules. So, I had an honest conversation with her and laid out what I expected going forward. It felt uncomfortable at first, but it was necessary. If I could share one thing with another guy, it would be this: establish your limits early. It saves you from a lot of unnecessary drama later on and helps you maintain your dignity.

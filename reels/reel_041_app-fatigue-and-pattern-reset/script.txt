I remember a time when I was swiping endlessly, thinking that the solution to my dating woes was just more profiles.

But honestly, it started to feel like a blur. I was just mindlessly scrolling, and I couldn’t even remember the last person I had a genuine conversation with. It was exhausting. Then, I decided to take a week off from the apps. At first, it felt weird—not being plugged in. But by the end of that break, I realized how numb I had become. It cleared my head and sharpened what I was looking for. Now, I’d tell any guy feeling burned out to step back for a bit. You’ll come back with fresh eyes and a better sense of what truly catches your interest.

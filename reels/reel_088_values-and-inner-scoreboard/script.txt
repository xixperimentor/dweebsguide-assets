I wasn’t living by any guiding principles; I was just getting easy attention. One night, I was set to meet someone I knew wouldn’t add real value to my life. If you’re feeling confused about a choice, ask yourself if your future self will respect your decision. Build your life around that vision, not just what's easy right now.

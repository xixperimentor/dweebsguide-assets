I remember this one date where I tried to be funny, but it kind of backfired.

I made a joke about her taste in music, thinking it would lighten the mood. Instead, I saw her face change. She looked taken aback, like I had hit a nerve. I realized that I’d crossed a line. It wasn’t playful; it felt mean-spirited to her. So, I took a step back. I apologized, shifted the tone, and focused on what we were enjoying together. I started to tease her about something harmless, and this time, it landed beautifully. So, my advice? Only tease when you’re already sharing a good vibe. If the energy shifts, drop it immediately. Make sure you’re both in on the fun. That's the key to keeping it light-hearted.

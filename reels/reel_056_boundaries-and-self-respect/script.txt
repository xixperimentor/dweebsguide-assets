There was a time when I found myself in a relationship that felt more confusing than fulfilling.

I kept dropping hints about wanting plans in advance, but all that did was create frustration for both of us. I realized I was hoping she'd just get it, but that only led to misunderstandings. One day, I decided to be straight with her. I simply said, “I need to know our plans ahead of time.” The relief was instant. Once I set that clear expectation, it opened up a real conversation between us. So if you're in a similar situation, don’t hesitate. Be direct about what you need. It’s not about being harsh; it’s about being honest. Good boundaries bring clarity, and that’s where real respect starts.

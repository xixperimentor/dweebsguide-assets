I remember early on in my dating life, I would meet someone and feel that rush, but it was always a bit chaotic.

I’d get excited about plans, but then they’d fall through. It kept me on edge, always second-guessing. I felt a mix of attraction and anxiety, and I eventually realized it was exhausting. Then I tried a different approach. I focused on being consistent. I made sure to follow through on my commitments and noticed how much that changed things. When I was steady, I could feel a shift. There was less tension and more genuine connection. For any guy out there, don’t underestimate the power of being reliable. It creates a safe space where attraction can grow without all the confusion. Just be the kind of man who shows up the way he says he will. It really makes a difference.

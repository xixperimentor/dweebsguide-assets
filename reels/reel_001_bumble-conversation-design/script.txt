I remember chatting with a girl on Bumble, thinking I needed to impress her with clever lines.

I piled on these witty messages, hoping they'd keep the conversation alive. But all I got back were short replies, and I felt the energy slip away. Then, I decided to switch it up. Instead of trying to be the funniest guy in the room, I made a simple observation about something we both liked. I followed that up with a straightforward question. Suddenly, the vibe shifted. It turned into a real back-and-forth where she felt comfortable sharing. If I could tell another guy one thing, it’d be this: keep it simple and give her a chance to engage. It’s more about balance than bravado.

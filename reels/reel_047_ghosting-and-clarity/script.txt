I remember this one time when I met a girl who seemed really into me.

We had great conversations, sparks flying. But then, out of nowhere, she just stopped replying. I took it pretty hard, thought I must’ve done something wrong. Looking back, I realized her silence said more about her than it did about me. Not everyone is equipped to handle the uncomfortable parts of dating. I let her ghosting turn into a personal judgment, which was a mistake. Now, I see things differently. When someone goes quiet, it’s really just a sign of their own issues, not a reflection of my worth. If it happens to you, don’t take it to heart. Instead, use it as a signal about their maturity, not your value.

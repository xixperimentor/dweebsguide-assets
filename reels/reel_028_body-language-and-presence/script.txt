A while back, I was on a date, and I could feel myself getting nervous as the conversation flowed.

I rushed my words, trying to fill every gap with chatter. I thought I was being engaging, but it just created this weird tension. I noticed her eyes flicker away, and I realized my voice was all over the place—high, fast, like I was asking for approval. So, I took a breath and slowed it down. I focused on letting my voice settle, making sure each word had weight. It changed everything. She started leaning in, smiling more, and the vibe transformed. If you find yourself in a similar spot, remember it’s not just about what you say. How you say it matters too. Take your time, breathe, and let your presence speak for itself.

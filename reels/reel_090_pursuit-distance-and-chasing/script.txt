I remember a time when I started dating someone new.

At first, I was drawn in by the mystery—she was elusive and kept me guessing. My mind was racing with all these scenarios, and I couldn’t help but feel excited by the uncertainty. But after a while, I realized I was focusing more on the games than on who she really was. It hit me that I was infatuated with the chase rather than the connection. I decided to pay closer attention to her actions, really looking at how she treated me compared to those wild fantasies I’d built up. So, if you find yourself caught up in the thrill of uncertainty, take a step back. Focus on the real interactions. You might discover there’s more to meaningful relationships than just the rush of the unknown.

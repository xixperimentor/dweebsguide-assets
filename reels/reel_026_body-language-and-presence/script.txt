I remember this one time I was out with someone I’d really wanted to impress.

I was leaning in hard, trying to be the center of her attention, thinking that would spark attraction. But honestly, it felt off. I could see her pulling back, and I realized my energy was too intense. Then it hit me—I was forcing it instead of just being myself. So, I took a breath, relaxed my shoulders, and let my smile be more natural. I focused on listening and enjoying the moment instead of trying to override it with pressure. If you find yourself in a similar spot, take a step back. Ease into the interaction and let it flow. Confidence comes from being grounded, not from trying too hard. Just be present.

I remember this one date where I was just skimming the surface.

We were chatting about the usual stuff—movies, work—but it felt like something was missing. I realized I was holding back, afraid to get too real too soon. Then, I thought, why not share something that actually mattered to me? So I told her about a time I felt lost during a big move. Her face lit up, and suddenly, we were connecting on a level I hadn’t expected. If I could give any guy advice, it’d be this: don’t shy away from genuine moments. Just swap out those generic responses for something grounded and specific. It shifts everything, trust me.

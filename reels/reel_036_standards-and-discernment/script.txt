I remember a time when I kept dating a woman who clearly didn’t value my time.

I thought if I just stuck it out, it would get better. But all I got was frustration. I was enduring, thinking that was a sign of strength. Then I started reflecting on what mattered to me. I realized I needed to set some standards—not to be rigid, but to protect my peace. I thought about what behaviors I simply wouldn’t accept anymore, like lack of communication and disrespect. Now, whenever I meet someone new, I’m clear about what I want. It’s not about being picky; it’s about being honest with myself and others. I’d tell any guy to think about his values and hold by them. It makes all the difference.

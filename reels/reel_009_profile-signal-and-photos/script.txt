I remember when I first started dating again after a long break.

My profile was all over the place—random facts, overly posed pictures. I thought I was playing it safe, but it was confusing for everyone. One day, a girl I really liked told me she felt like she didn’t know who I was. That hit hard. It made me realize I was missing the mark. I had to rethink everything. I started using photos that showed me in my real life—hiking with friends, cooking at home, laughing in a bar. Now, I ask myself: “What do these images say about me?” I’d tell any guy to focus on authenticity over approval. The right person will see you clearly.

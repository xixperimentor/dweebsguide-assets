Let me tell you about a time I was chatting on a dating app.

I was stuck in this endless back-and-forth, trying to impress her with clever quips and stories. I thought if I just kept the conversation going, she’d warm up to me. But after a while, I realized I was just killing time instead of seeing if we actually clicked. One day, I decided to shift gears. I asked her to grab coffee, confident that if the vibe was right, a straightforward invite wouldn’t scare her off. It turned out I was right. When the exchange felt easy, it was clear she was interested. So, don’t drag it out. If it’s flowing well, don’t hesitate to suggest meeting up. You’ll know soon enough if it’s worth your time.

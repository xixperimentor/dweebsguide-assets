I remember this one time when I started dating someone new.

The chemistry was electric, and I felt this urge to define things too soon. I thought, “This is it,” and pushed for a label after just a couple of amazing dates. But honestly, I hadn’t paused to consider if we had built any real connection yet. So, I took a step back. I focused on simply enjoying our time together without the pressure of titles. Over the next few weeks, I noticed how our interactions evolved naturally; we had some great conversations and laughs. When the moment felt right, I brought it up again. If I could give advice, it would be to let things develop organically. Enjoy the journey instead of rushing to conclusions. Sometimes, the best connections grow from a solid foundation, not just high energy at the start.

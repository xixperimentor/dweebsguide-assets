I remember a time early on when I was really into this woman.

She wanted to meet up last minute, and I dropped everything. I thought being super available would impress her. Instead, I ended up feeling drained and a bit resentful. One day, I paused and realized I was bending over backward just to keep her interested. I began respecting my own time more. I set some boundaries, like sticking to my gym schedule and not changing my plans for a casual hangout. Now, I encourage other guys to think about their own routines. It’s tough at first, but saying no here and there actually shows you value yourself. Trust me, women notice that.

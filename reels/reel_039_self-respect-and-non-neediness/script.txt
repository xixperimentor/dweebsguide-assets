There was a time when I was really into this woman I met at a party.

I liked her, but I felt anxious about putting myself out there. Instead of just saying I wanted to see her again, I played it cool. It didn't work. When I finally got the courage to be direct, I realized I was loaded with all this pressure, trying to gauge her every reaction. What I learned was that it’s about expressing interest without needing her to validate my feelings. So, I decided to just say, “I’d love to grab a drink sometime.” Simple, honest, and no strings attached. If I could give any advice, it would be this: own your feelings but don’t let them own you. Confidence comes from a place of self-respect, not desperation.

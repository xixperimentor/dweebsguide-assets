A little while back, I was on a date that felt like it was going great—until I made a comment that seemed to throw her off.

I could see her expression change; something about my tone just didn’t hit right. Instead of brushing it off, I paused and really thought about it. Instead of defending my words, I took a breath and said, “Hey, I didn’t mean it that way. I’m sorry if it came off wrong.” The moment I owned up to it, the tension lifted. She appreciated that I recognized my part in it. If I could share one thing with another guy, it’d be this: don’t let your pride get in the way. A quick acknowledgment of a slip can turn things around and build that trust. It really matters.

I remember a time when I got really caught up with a girl I barely knew.

I'd find myself checking my phone constantly, feeling that buzz of anxiety every time she didn’t respond right away. I thought that intensity meant we had something special, but deep down, I knew the connection was shaky. Then, I took a step back and realized my obsession wasn’t about her; it was more about my own insecurities. I was filling a void inside myself, hoping she’d somehow make everything okay. So, I started focusing on what really mattered—my own life and confidence. I learned to appreciate the moments we had but without chasing after her constant approval. If I could tell another guy dealing with this, I’d say to take a breath. Slow down, and really ask yourself if what you're feeling is about them or about you.

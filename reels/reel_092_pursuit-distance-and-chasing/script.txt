There was this one time I was really into a woman.

I found myself always being the one to text first, plan our outings, and carry the conversation. At first, I thought I was doing everything right, but it felt off. I was putting in all the effort, and it was draining. Then I took a step back and realized I was the only one fueling this thing. I decided to change my approach. I let her take the lead a bit more and focused on matching her energy. What I learned was that a relationship thrives on both sides engaging. So, if you're ever in that position, don’t carry the load alone. Watch for her interest and let it flow both ways. Trust me, it makes all the difference.

I remember a time when I was chatting with a girl I really liked.

Every time I suggested getting together, it was always vague—“maybe we should hang out sometime.” After a few back-and-forths, I realized we were just stuck in limbo, neither of us knowing what to do next. Then it hit me. One day, I decided to be direct. I texted her a specific plan: “How about we grab coffee at that spot downtown this Saturday at 3 PM?” She responded almost instantly, and I felt this huge weight lift. It wasn’t just about the coffee; it was about clarity. I learned that being straightforward shows confidence. So, my advice? When you invite her, be direct and don’t dance around it. Clarity opens doors.

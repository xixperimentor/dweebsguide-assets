I remember dating a girl who had this amazing energy.

I was drawn to her, but soon I found myself shifting my opinions just to keep things smooth. I noticed I was altering my own preferences to fit what she liked instead of being myself. It hit me one night when I was pretending to be into things I wasn’t. I realized I was losing sight of who I truly was, and that’s just not sustainable. So, I took a step back. I started being real about my interests and values, and I could feel the weight lift. If you ever find yourself bending too far just to please someone, take a moment. Ask yourself if this connection is expanding you or forcing you to shrink. Your authenticity is what makes you attractive in the first place.

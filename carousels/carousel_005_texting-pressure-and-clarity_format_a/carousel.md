# Carousel 005: TEXTING PRESSURE AND CLARITY

Source concept: C005
Format: format_a
Voices: Logan Ury, Mark Manson

## Slide 1 - PRINCIPLE

Hook: Texting gets worse when you try to compress chemistry, reassurance, and planning into one message.

Body: Each message works better when it has one job: continue the vibe, clarify intent, or move logistics forward.

## Slide 2 - COMMON MISTAKE

Hook: Blending flirting, overexplaining, and scheduling in the same text makes you sound crowded.

Body: One clean signal is stronger than three mixed signals fighting for room in the same sentence.

## Slide 3 - REFRAME

Hook: One clean signal is stronger than three mixed signals fighting for room in the same sentence.

Body: A warm check-in and a separate plan message often feels calmer than a sprawling monologue that does both badly.

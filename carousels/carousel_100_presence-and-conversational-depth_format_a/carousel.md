# Carousel 100: PRESENCE AND CONVERSATIONAL DEPTH

Source concept: C100
Format: format_a
Voices: Esther Perel, Vanessa Van Edwards

## Slide 1 - PRINCIPLE

Hook: Curiosity feels attractive when it is generous rather than investigative.

Body: People open up more when your interest feels spacious and alive, not like an attempt to extract the right answer.

## Slide 2 - COMMON MISTAKE

Hook: Anxious curiosity can come off like pressure because it chases momentum instead of welcoming discovery.

Body: Let curiosity unfold at the pace of trust rather than at the pace of your impatience.

## Slide 3 - REFRAME

Hook: Let curiosity unfold at the pace of trust rather than at the pace of your impatience.

Body: A thoughtful pause and one sincere follow-up often feels better than four questions fired in a row.

Source concept: C015

## Slide 1 - PRINCIPLE

Hook: Confidence on a date often looks like tempo control more than verbal brilliance.

Body: People feel steadier around someone who is not rushing the interaction to prove something.

## Slide 2 - COMMON MISTAKE

Hook: Filling every silence can make you miss the information that natural pauses are trying to give you.

Body: Slow enough to notice, warm enough to invite, direct enough to lead.

## Slide 3 - REFRAME

Hook: Slow enough to notice, warm enough to invite, direct enough to lead.

Body: A short pause, eye contact, and a thoughtful follow-up can feel more grounded than nervous chatter.

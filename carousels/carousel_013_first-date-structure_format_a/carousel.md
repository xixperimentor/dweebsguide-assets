# Carousel 013: FIRST DATE STRUCTURE

Source concept: C013
Format: format_a
Voices: Logan Ury, Esther Perel

## Slide 1 - PRINCIPLE

Hook: A first date works better when the format leaves room for movement and lightness.

Body: Dates feel easier when they allow conversation to build gradually instead of trapping both people in high stakes immediately.

## Slide 2 - COMMON MISTAKE

Hook: Overdesigning the plan can create the exact tension you were trying to avoid.

Body: Think progression, not spectacle: a simple setting with options is usually enough.

## Slide 3 - REFRAME

Hook: Think progression, not spectacle: a simple setting with options is usually enough.

Body: Coffee, a walk, and the option to continue creates more usable chemistry than a long formal dinner with no exits.

## Slide 4 - EXAMPLE

Hook: Coffee, a walk, and the option to continue creates more usable chemistry than a long formal dinner with no exits.

Body: Choose first-date plans that are easy to extend when the vibe is good and easy to end when it is not.

# Carousel 003: BUMBLE CONVERSATION DESIGN

Source concept: C003

Voices: Logan Ury, Vanessa Van Edwards

## Slide 1 - PRINCIPLE

Hook: Questions work best when they reveal your perspective, not when they read like an interview.

Body: A question becomes attractive when it gives the other person something interesting to react to, not just something to answer.

## Slide 2 - COMMON MISTAKE

Hook: Rapid-fire prompts can make you seem anxious for momentum instead of present in the moment.

Body: Lead with a specific thought, then ask a question that opens a lane for personality rather than compliance.

## Slide 3 - REFRAME

Hook: Lead with a specific thought, then ask a question that opens a lane for personality rather than compliance.

Body: Saying what caught your attention first and then asking about it creates a more human exchange than generic small talk.

## Slide 4 - EXAMPLE

Hook: Saying what caught your attention first and then asking about it creates a more human exchange than generic small talk.

## Slide 1 - PRINCIPLE

Hook: A second date should usually answer whether the first-date interest has real follow-through, not just replay the same uncertainty.

Body: The second meeting is where tone, effort, and compatibility often become easier to read than they were under first-date nerves.

Hook: Many men either chase a second date automatically or rule it out too fast based on one imperfect first impression.

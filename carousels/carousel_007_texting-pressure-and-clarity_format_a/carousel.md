## Slide 1 - PRINCIPLE

Hook: Response time is weak evidence unless you read it with context.

Body: People can be interested and still be busy, distracted, awkward, or inconsistent over text.

## Slide 2 - COMMON MISTAKE

Hook: Treating every delay like a hidden message turns normal uncertainty into self-inflicted spiraling.

Body: Look for patterns across several interactions rather than trying to decode one delayed reply like a verdict.

## Slide 3 - REFRAME

Hook: Look for patterns across several interactions rather than trying to decode one delayed reply like a verdict.

## Slide 1 - PRINCIPLE

Hook: Your job early on is not to entertain nonstop; it is to make the interaction feel easy.

Body: People stay engaged when the exchange feels light enough to continue without social strain.

## Slide 2 - COMMON MISTAKE

Hook: Trying to prove value in every message often creates pressure faster than attraction.

Body: Aim for comfort plus intrigue: enough warmth to feel safe, enough personality to feel distinct.

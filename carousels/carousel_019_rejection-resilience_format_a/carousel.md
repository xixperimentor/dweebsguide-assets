## Slide 1 - PRINCIPLE

Hook: Emotional sturdiness comes from tolerating uncertainty without converting it into cynicism.

Body: The temptation after a few bad experiences is to become sarcastic, detached, or contemptuous, but that costs you the very openness you need.

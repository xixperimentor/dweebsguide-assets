# Carousel 077: EARLY RELATIONSHIP PROGRESSION

Source concept: C077
Format: format_a
Voices: Logan Ury, Gottman Institute

## Slide 1 - PRINCIPLE

Hook: Early exclusivity conversations go better when they are built on patterns, not pressure.

Body: You do not need to guess where the relationship is going forever, but you do need enough real consistency before naming it heavily.

## Slide 2 - COMMON MISTAKE

Hook: Some men ask for certainty too early because uncertainty feels unbearable, not because the relationship is actually ready.

Body: Good timing means enough shared experience has accumulated to make the conversation real rather than aspirational.

## Slide 3 - REFRAME

Hook: Good timing means enough shared experience has accumulated to make the conversation real rather than aspirational.

Body: Several weeks of mutual effort, clear contact, and stable interest provide a sturdier base than one intense stretch of momentum.
